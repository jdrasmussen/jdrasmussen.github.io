Voter ID in Wisconsin

I developed this infographic for an assignment in my Graphic Design in Cartography course in my master's program. The assignment was to create a long-form infographic that would use at least one bi-variate map. To do this, I used data from both the U.S. Census Bureau and from the Wisconsin Legislative Technology Services Bureau, which I processed in ArcGIS and Adobe Illustrator to create the maps in the graphic below.
