Interactive Map of Zion National park

I created this interactive map of Zion National Park to allow prospective visitors to explore the park's offerings in preparation for a trip. The page allows visitors to search campgrounds and trails in the park, and filter trails by the National Park Service's difficulty rating. This project was for the capstone in GIS Development course at UW-Madison.
