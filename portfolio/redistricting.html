Interactive Redistricting Vizualization

I created this interactive visualization to explore the impact of redistricting on the distribution of safe vs. toss-up legislative districts in Wisconsin, in the wake of controversial redistricting by the GOP after the 2010 Census. This tool allows the user to select an election to use as a baseline for voter preferences and then move between different district lines to see how the distribution of legislative seats changes. I do not have this application currently hosted or published anywhere online.
