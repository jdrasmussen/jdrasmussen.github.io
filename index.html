About Me

I'm an IT professional, cartographer, and GIS specialist based in Madison, WI where I live with my wife and our two cats. Currently I work at a credit union here in Madison as a system administrator in the IT department and am working on my Master's in GIS and Web Map Programming.

I received my bachelor's degree in Geography and Urban Studies from Macalester College in 2007. I've got a passion for using geographic data and visualizations to tell stories and understand the world that surrounds us. Contact me if you've got a project that requires organization and display of geographic data to reach it's full potential.
